Technology

The right AI model for the job

We don't default to one platform. We select the AI model that best fits your use case, budget, and data privacy requirements.

GPT-4o
Best All-Round

Ideal for complex reasoning, customer-facing chat, document analysis, and content generation. Our default for most client-facing applications.

Claude (Anthropic)
Long Documents

Excellent for long-document analysis, nuanced professional writing, and safety-sensitive applications. Strong extended context window.

Gemini
Multimodal

Strong with text, image, and audio together. Best fit for clients already in the Google Workspace ecosystem.

Open Source
Privacy-First

Llama, Mistral, and others. Best for clients with strict data privacy requirements or on-premise needs. No per-token API costs post-deployment.

Specialist Models
Domain-Specific

Purpose-built models for legal, medical, or financial industries where fine-tuned accuracy in a regulated field is critical.

Integration Platforms

How we connect AI to your existing tools

Most AI solutions need to connect to your booking system, CRM, email, or accounting software. We use proven platforms to make those connections.

Make (Integromat)
Primary automation platform

Our default platform for most clients. Visual, reliable, and connects 1,000+ apps. Excellent for SMB workflow automation.

Zapier
Simpler automations

Used when the client already has a Zapier subscription or when the use case is straightforward and Make is overkill.

n8n
Self-hosted option

Open-source automation for clients wanting full control and self-hosted workflows. Requires more technical setup.

Voiceflow / Botpress
Chatbot builders

Purpose-built conversation designers. Used when a visual conversation flow speeds up delivery on chatbot projects.

Custom Python / API
When no platform fits

When no no-code platform supports the required integration, or when performance and flexibility are critical.

Railway / Render
Lightweight cloud hosting

Simple cloud hosting for lightweight custom applications and APIs. Low cost, easy deployment, easy maintenance.

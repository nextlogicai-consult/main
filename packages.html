Our Pricing Philosophy

We price on value, not hours

How we price reflects how we think about our work — and it matters for how we'll treat your project.

💡

Expertise over execution

If an automation saves your team $3,000/month in labour, a project fee isn't expensive — it pays back in weeks. You're paying for judgment about which solution is actually right.

🎯

One recommendation, not a menu

We never present three tiers and ask you to pick. After discovery, we recommend the single right package for your situation — and we explain why.

📉

Simpler over sophisticated

A solution your team can actually maintain beats a complex one they can't. We will never propose a more elaborate system than your situation genuinely requires.

🤝

No goodwill discounts

We don't reduce price for the same scope. If budget is tight, we reduce scope to match — maintaining quality on a smaller, better-defined project.

🔒

Transparent terms

50% deposit to begin, 50% at launch. All agreements in writing. Change orders documented before any work starts. No surprises at invoice time.

🌱

Built to grow

Every project is built with the assumption that it may expand. Clean architecture, version-controlled prompts, and documented integrations mean additions are straightforward.

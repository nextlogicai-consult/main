Our Pricing Philosophy

A few things worth knowing about how we price

How we price reflects how we think about the work. It's worth understanding before we scope anything together.

💡

You're paying for judgment, not hours

If an automation saves your team $3,000 a month in labour, the project fee pays for itself in weeks. What you're really investing in is knowing which solution is actually worth building.

🎯

One recommendation, not a menu

We never present three tiers and ask you to pick. After the discovery call, we tell you what we'd build and why. It's the same thing we'd build if it were our own business.

📉

Simpler beats sophisticated

A system your team can actually maintain is worth more than a complex one they quietly avoid. We will never propose more than your situation genuinely needs.

🤝

We reduce scope, not quality

If budget is tighter than the scope, we build less, not the same thing with corners cut. The smaller project gets the same care and attention as anything else we do.

🔒

Transparent terms

50% deposit to begin, 50% at launch. Everything in writing. Change orders documented before any work starts. No surprises at invoice time.

🌱

Built to grow with you

Every project is built with the assumption it may expand. Clean architecture, version-controlled prompts, and documented integrations mean adding to it later is straightforward.

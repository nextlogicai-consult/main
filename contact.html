What actually happens on this call

It's not a sales call. We won't show you a deck or spend 40 minutes explaining what AI is. We're going to ask you a lot of questions about your business, listen carefully, and at the end give you an honest read on whether what you're describing is something AI can actually help with.

Sometimes the answer is yes and we can start scoping something right away. Sometimes the answer is "not yet" and we'll tell you what would need to change first. Either way, you leave the call knowing something useful.

Being Upfront

We're not the right fit for everyone

We'd rather tell you that now than waste both our time. Here's a straightforward look at who we work well with and who we probably don't.

We're probably a good fit if...

  • You have a specific, repeatable problem that's costing you time every week
  • Your team is willing to learn and adapt to new tools
  • You want a long-term partner, not just someone to hand you a manual and disappear
  • You'd rather start small and prove value before scaling up
  • You care more about something that actually works than something that looks impressive

We're probably not the right fit if...

  • You need something built in 48 hours with no planning involved
  • Your data is completely disorganized and you're not ready to address that yet
  • You want to hand off a problem and never think about it again
  • You're shopping purely on price regardless of the outcome
  • You need something outside our core service areas
Common Questions

Things people ask before booking

If something is stopping you from filling out the form, the answer is probably here.

Do I need to know anything about AI before the call?

Not at all. We work with business owners who have never touched an AI tool and with people who have already tried a few things and gotten stuck. Either way, we start from where you are, not where we assume you should be.

What if I'm not sure AI is even the right solution?

That's one of the most common things we hear. Honestly, figuring out whether AI is the right tool is half the point of the discovery call. Sometimes it is. Sometimes a simpler fix makes more sense and we'll tell you that directly.

Is this actually free or is there a catch?

It's genuinely free. No credit card, no trial, nothing to cancel. We do discovery calls because they're the only honest way to figure out if there's a real fit. We'd rather invest 45 minutes upfront than take on a project that isn't right for either of us.

How quickly can we get started after the call?

If there's a clear fit, we can usually have a project brief in front of you within 2 to 3 days. Timelines from there depend on scope. Starter projects typically run 1 to 2 weeks and Builder projects run 2 to 4 weeks from signed brief to launch.

What does it cost?

We don't publish fixed prices because scope varies too much for that to be honest. What we can tell you is that we price on the value delivered, not hours spent, and we'll never recommend a bigger engagement than your situation actually calls for. You'll have full pricing in writing before anything gets started.

What if I just want to ask a quick question first?

Email us at info@nextlogicai.com and we'll get back to you within 4 business hours. No question is too basic and we won't try to turn every email into a sales conversation.
